BMI calculator

The body mass index (BMI) is a measure of someone's weight in relation to their height. It is often used to check whether they have a healthy weight. A BMI greater than 25 could signal that one is overweight and a BMI greater than 30 could signal obesity. Some people use this as an indicator to make dietary adjustments towards healthier living.
